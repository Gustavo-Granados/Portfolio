How to get Started

To get into the hobby of playing Video Games, you'll need to get some supplies, and understand your taste. There's a game for every genre, but it is best to have an idea of what you perfer before you start buying them.

Required equipment

  • Audio System [Headphones, Speakers, etc.]
  • Input Device [Mouse&Keyboard, Controller, Wii Remote, etc.]
  • Monitor [Computer Screen, TV, etc.]
  • Gaming System [Console, PC, Souljagame]

Consoles and PCs

A gaming console is a computer system specifically made for the purpose of playing Video Games. A piece of hardware that can play the newer and more demanding games. Some of the more recent consoles include;

  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Xbox One X

Though consoles are not the only option to download and play video games, another option is Personal Computers. There are online platforms where people can buy and download games to their computer. These online markets include platforms such as;

  • Steam
  • Uplay
  • Epic Games Store
  • Origin
