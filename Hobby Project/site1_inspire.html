My Insperation

I got into Video Games when I was 8 years old, playing WII Sports on the Nintendo WII in my basement at the time. I was always facinated by how a button I pressed or a movement I made could effect what happened on the screen. Though I must say that most of my experience at the time came from going to a friends house, where he was allowed to play for longer and owned more games than I did. This was around the time when the massive indie game MineCraft was launched, and from then on I played any game I could get my hands on. The main contributors to this interest were the hordes of Gaming Youtubers. People who recorded themselves playing games I liked while providing what was considered 'comedic commentary' at the time. In recent times I've narrowed down my preference, and play a handful of games that I've enjoyed for a while. Overall, this has been my longest lasting hobby that I can enjoy whenever I think about it.
