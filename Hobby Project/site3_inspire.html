Chess

I started playing chess when I first moved to Arizona as a pass-time when I was not allowed to play Video Games. Although I used it as a filler hobby, I enjoyed it just the same as anything else I was doing at the time. When I visited my grandpa in California he offered to play a game against me for fun. He of course, beat me any time I tried to play him. From that point on I had a purpose, to defeat the grandparent that had bested me in a game that I only played in my free time. Currently I have not beaten him yet, but the thought that one day I will keeps me determined.
